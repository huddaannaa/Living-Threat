Help & Guide Living MITRE Repository

Platform description

The Living MITRE Repository is a threat-intelligence platform that transforms extracted security incident reports into a Navigator-style MITRE ATT&CK view. Incidents are aggregated, correlated, and mapped to ATT&CK tactics and techniques, allowing analysts to move from raw reporting to structured, actionable threat context.

Technique cards and report linkage

Under each tactic, the platform displays technique cards. Each card represents a MITRE ATT&CK technique associated with one or more real incident reports. Cards show technique ID, technique name, short description, ranking/weight indicators, and a report icon that opens mapped incidents. Selecting a report highlights the ATT&CK technique chain for that incident across the Navigator.

How the platform works

Step 1: Tailor the view
Filter by severity, actor, tool, country, sector, software, breach type, access vector, and other facets.

Step 2: Select time range
Choose the reporting window that fits your investigation.

Step 3: Review the Navigator
Technique cards are arranged by ATT&CK tactic and prioritized visually (Red=Rank 1, Yellow=Rank 2, Blue=Rank 3).

Step 4: Select an incident report
Selecting a report highlights the ATT&CK technique flow for that specific incident in the Navigator.

Step 5: Open Attack Path
View the incident through the Cyber Kill Chain for stage-by-stage operational context.

Step 6: Open Hunt Pack
Get deeper investigative guidance, context, and remediation-oriented information.

Step 7: Continue, refresh, reset, or share
Reset state, review newly available data, use Refresh to load updates, and Share to copy the exact current view for collaboration.
